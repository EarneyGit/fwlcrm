Privacy Policy for FWL CRM

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

1. Introduction

FWL CRM ("we," "us," or "our") operates this application to help agencies manage leads. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service, specifically concerning data received from Meta platforms.

2. Data We Collect

When you authorize our Meta App, we collect the following data:

  • Lead Information: Names, emails, phone numbers, and custom questions submitted by users via Facebook and Instagram Lead Ads.
  • Page Information: Basic public profile information about the Facebook Pages you connect.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To sync incoming leads from Meta to your FWL CRM dashboard.
  • To allow you (the agency) to manage, contact, and organize leads.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data or your leads' data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is transmitted securely via HTTPS and stored in a secure PostgreSQL database. We implement strict access controls to ensure that only authorized agents within your organization can view lead data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you and to request its deletion. For data deletion instructions, please see our Data Deletion Policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@futurewavelabs.com.
