User Data Deletion Instructions

FWL CRM provides a mechanism for users to request the deletion of their data in compliance with Meta's platform rules and GDPR/CCPA requirements.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you wish to remove your Facebook Page connection or delete the lead data stored by our app, you can do so by following these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's Settings & Privacy.
  2. Click on Settings.
  3. Navigate to Security and Login and click on Business Integrations.
  4. Find FWL CRM in the list of active integrations.
  5. Click Remove to revoke our app's access to your Facebook account.

If you want us to completely erase all historical lead data and account information from our databases, please send an email to privacy@futurewavelabs.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request and permanently delete your data within 7 business days.
